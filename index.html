I · About

I believe the best marketing isn't manipulation — it's communication with purpose.
My work lives at the intersection of strategy, story, and the deeper forces that move people.

My background spans nonprofit marketing, content strategy, operations management, and educational writing. Alongside my professional work, I'm drawn to Jungian psychology, mythology, symbolism, and the archetypal structures beneath every story that endures.

These worlds intersect in everything I create. Whether I'm building campaigns, writing editorial content, or exploring depth psychology, the question is always the same: what meaning is this making, and for whom?
